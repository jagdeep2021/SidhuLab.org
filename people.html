Principal Investigator

Dr. Jagdeep Singh Sidhu

Principal Investigator

Jagdeep Singh Sidhu is a Postdoctoral Scholar at South Dakota State University, researching root phenotypes and host genetics under abiotic stress. He is passionate about developing climate-resilient crops to address agricultural challenges in the face of climate change.

About

Sidhu Lab explores how plant biology, data science, and climate resilience intersect—translating complex root and microbial responses into insights for sustainable agriculture.

Our research identifies traits that improve crop tolerance to drought and nutrient stress, using phenotyping and predictive modeling for resilient crop development.

Our Guiding Principles

At Sidhu Lab, our mission is rooted in the belief that data is a tool for transformation. We apply advanced computational methods.

Our work thrives at the intersection of disciplines. We integrate plant physiology, genetics, engineering, and machine learning to tackle some of agriculture’s most pressing challenges.

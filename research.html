Welcome to DeepLab Research

Unlocking the secrets of plant resilience through the lens of structural-functional
biology, evolutionary dynamics, and adaptive responses to abiotic stress.

Major Themes in Our Work

Multiscale Structural-functional Biology

We study plant structures at cellular, tissue, and organ levels.

Abiotic Stress Resilience

We focus on traits that help crops survive drought, heat, and nutrient stress to improve yields.

Evolutionary Biology

By studying plant evolution and wild ancestors, we reintroduce useful traits for modern crop performance.

Local Adaptation

We analyze how crops adapt to soil, temperature, and rainfall conditions to develop region-specific varieties.
